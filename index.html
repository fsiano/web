Federico Siano

Welcome to my web page! I am an Assistant Professor of Accounting at The University of Texas at Dallas.

I specialize in empirical capital markets research. I study the decision-relevance of unstructured information within corporate disclosures. I am particularly interested in the interplay between numerical and textual information within the accounting system. My research applies “big-data” methods to empirical economic questions.

Tags: Capital Markets, Disclosure, Textual Analysis, Big Data/AI

In 2022, I received my PhD in Accounting from Boston University. Prior to this, I studied business economics, finance, and accounting at the London School of Economics and Bocconi University.

Before joining Academia, I spent four years in the financial sector as an investment and consulting professional.
